Editorial Policy

Last Updated: December 18, 2024

At WellGuide Health, our editorial integrity is non-negotiable. This policy explains how we create content, review providers, and maintain independence in all our recommendations.

Our Editorial Mission

We exist to provide accurate, unbiased, and helpful information about online GLP-1 weight loss programs. Our goal is to help you make informed decisions about your health by cutting through marketing claims and presenting the facts.

Editorial Independence

Our reviews and rankings are never influenced by affiliate commissions or advertising relationships.

Here's how we maintain independence:

  • Editors Have Final Say: Our editorial team makes all decisions about content, rankings, and recommendations—not our business or affiliate partners
  • No Pay-for-Play: Providers cannot pay to improve their ranking or receive better reviews
  • We Report Problems: If a provider has issues, we report them honestly—even if it means losing affiliate revenue
  • Regular Updates: We update reviews when providers change, even if updates reflect negatively on partners

Our Review Process

Every provider review follows our comprehensive evaluation framework:

1. Medical Safety & Legitimacy (30%)

  • Verification of licensed healthcare providers
  • Required health screenings and lab work
  • FDA approval status of medications
  • Pharmacy accreditation and standards
  • Medical oversight and follow-up protocols

2. Pricing & Transparency (25%)

  • Total monthly cost analysis
  • Hidden fee identification
  • Insurance acceptance and support
  • Refund and cancellation policies
  • Value relative to competitors

3. User Experience & Support (20%)

  • Ease of signup and onboarding
  • Customer service quality and availability
  • Provider communication and responsiveness
  • Platform usability (website/app)
  • Analysis of real user reviews across multiple platforms

4. Medication Access & Options (15%)

  • Medication variety (Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, etc.)
  • Brand-name vs. compounded options
  • Shipping speed and reliability
  • Dosing flexibility

5. Long-Term Success Support (10%)

  • Coaching and lifestyle support availability
  • Nutritional guidance
  • Exercise programs or recommendations
  • Community support features
  • Maintenance phase planning

Content Standards

Accuracy

  • All medical information is verified against reputable sources
  • Pricing information is confirmed through direct provider research
  • Statistics and data include source citations
  • Claims about effectiveness reference clinical studies when available

Objectivity

  • We present both pros and cons for every provider
  • Rankings reflect comprehensive evaluation, not commission rates
  • We disclose limitations in our knowledge or research
  • We avoid sensational or misleading headlines

Clarity

  • Medical terminology is explained in plain language
  • Complex topics are broken down into understandable sections
  • Pricing breakdowns are clear and detailed
  • Affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed

Content Creation Process

Research Phase

  1. Comprehensive provider research (website, policies, user reviews)
  2. Direct communication with provider customer service
  3. Analysis of user reviews across multiple platforms
  4. Verification of medical credentials and licensing
  5. Comparison with competitor offerings

Writing Phase

  1. Content drafted based on research findings
  2. Medical information verified against authoritative sources
  3. Pricing and policies confirmed with providers
  4. Pros and cons objectively presented

Review Phase

  1. Editorial review for accuracy and completeness
  2. Fact-checking of all claims and statistics
  3. Verification that affiliate disclosures are present and clear
  4. Final approval before publication

Updates and Corrections

Regular Updates

  • Provider reviews are updated quarterly or when significant changes occur
  • Pricing information is verified and updated monthly
  • User review analysis refreshed every 2-3 months
  • New providers added as they meet our evaluation standards

Corrections Policy

If we discover an error in our content:

  • We correct it immediately
  • We note the correction at the bottom of the article
  • For significant errors, we add an editor's note at the top of the article
  • We never delete content to hide mistakes

What We Don't Do

  • Accept Payment for Reviews: Providers cannot pay us to be reviewed or featured
  • Guarantee Rankings: We don't promise providers specific rankings or placements
  • Suppress Negative Information: If a provider has problems, we report them
  • Recommend Unsafe Providers: Providers that don't meet safety standards are not featured
  • Provide Medical Advice: We educate and inform, but never diagnose or prescribe

Feedback and Accountability

We welcome feedback on our content and editorial practices:

  • Report errors or outdated information: contact@wellguidehealth.com
  • Share your experience with providers we review
  • Suggest improvements to our review methodology
  • Ask questions about how we evaluate providers
